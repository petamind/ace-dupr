Rating Algorithm

Ratings are computed using a DUPR-approximation Elo algorithm with score-differential performance, K-factor decay (K_max=0.40, scale=20 matches), 180-day recency half-life, and match type multipliers (tournament 1.5×, club 1.0×, recreational 0.5×). New players start at 3.500. Provisional until 3+ matches in 90 days.
